About Youth for Christ

Youth for Christ was formed on 5 February 2021 through a vision given by God to Abinjose JM. It started with online sessions like fasting prayers, Bible quizzes, and games. Over time, it grew spiritually with the guidance of Christ and the Holy Spirit.

In 2023, we formed a leadership team known as the Heads — 13 young leaders committed to the vision. Youth for Christ hosted its first major offline camp "Megalono 2024" with the theme “Expanding the Territory”, which was a great success.

We also conducted a Gospel Social Preach on Independence Day (15 August 2024) and launched a Bible quiz named Logos. Now, we look forward to Megalono 3.0, the next edition of Logos, and more gospel-centered events.

Our mission: To uplift youth, spread the gospel, and create opportunities for the next generation to Grow Up in Christ.

Assaphians – Musical Band of Youth for Christ

"Praise the Lord with harp: sing unto Him with the psaltery and an instrument of ten strings." – Psalm 33:2

Assaphians is the official worship band of Youth for Christ, launched on 23rd February 2023. Inspired by Asaph in the Bible, the band exists to glorify God through music and worship.

It provides a platform for youth with musical talents to express their faith and worship through instruments and song. From praise nights to gospel events, Assaphians aims to bring people closer to Christ.

Our mission: To give youngsters opportunities in the music field to praise God and use their talents for His glory.
